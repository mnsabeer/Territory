What Is STDB

The Site To Do Business, or STDB, is a veteran market leader in commercial real estate demographics. Over the past 15 years, the technology has developed to best serve the needs of commercial real estate professionals across the country. By integrating online data into a dynamic GIS mapping environment, the site allows users to competently and consistently make the best decision possible for a given piece of real estate. By relying on the best data for decision making, STDB users constantly position themselves to earn business. Using STDB provides commercial real estate professionals the reliable and singular source for real estate analytical tools such as demographic data, business information, thematic mapping, lifestyle data and high quality aerial imagery. Xceligent, as the service provider for CCIMTECH, will be integrating many of the geospatial analysis tools of STDB with their products to allow for detailed analysis of commercial properties. STDB is a wholly owned subsidiary of the CCIM Institute.

The web-based application provides access to a variety of databases and includes:

  • Demographic Reports
  • Color-Coded Thematic Maps
  • Custom Comparison Reports
  • Retail Market Potential
  • Smart Maps o PDF Mapping
  • Tapestry Segmentation
  • Traffic Count Data
  • Custom Variables
  • Consumer Spending
  • Satellite Imagery
  • Mobile Applications
  • Business Information
  • Map Annotations
